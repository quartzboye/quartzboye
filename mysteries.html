1. Eli Stroud Cemetery


Uncle Eli Stroud "rests" here in Salem, AL. I say he still hunts deer in his buckskin suit. They should have never dug him up decades ago. It's cold with a misty type fog there. My friend saw a "man" on Ghost Town Road across the street near Alter Rock years back. I've always felt safe there I guess because he is family after all. Please respect the cemetery and graves if you visit. His full history is posted elsewhere online if you are interested in him. (Photo is mine)
